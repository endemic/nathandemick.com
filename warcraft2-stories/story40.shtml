Shlonglor's War 2 Stories
Gotcha and His wacky stuff.

Last Updated 4/16/96
Gotcha and I joined a game. Harrier was serving it. He started saying Shlonglor and Gotcha, you guys are legends! That was so funny. Well he made the mistake of picking high resources. This was a non patch game. I got a second town hall instantly. I was producing so much stuff it was unbelievable. When you got high resources, there is no reason not to.

Anyway Gotcha decided to go block skyking or skyderz or skysomeone, I forgot his name, into his town. If you block that one entrance, black is stuck. Gotcha built his lumber mill and barracks. I easily took care of Harrier. Gotcha spent the whole time on Skywhoever. It was a very easy game but what made it cool was what Gotcha did to Skywhoever. These three pictures tell the whole story.

Gotcha sent a few peons over to blacks town. I think he put up these two towers then upgraded them at the same time. Gotcha built this wall here. When Skywhoever saw gotcha, he sent his footman to attack. Gotcha quickly filled the hole up with that farm you see building there. The towers quickly took out sky's footman. You can see their dead bodies. Sky started repairing his barracks. hahaha. You never repair stuff you have no chance of saving :)

When Gotcha's peon got done building the farm, he popped out inside the wall. Sky gave up repairing the barracks. Gotcha used his peon and tower to take down the barracks.

Gotcha rolled in a catapult from his home town. He built a barracks inside the wall in. He built those 2 archers and footman you see. He also built that 3rd tower. I think that was all it took to take Skywhoever out. It was so funny. He couldn't leave his village. It was perfect. I was laughing so hard. They guy never got a chance to do anything at all. muhahahaha.

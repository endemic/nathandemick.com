Drunken Dor

Last Updated 10/23/97

This had to be one of the more funny stories. Dor had it it out for me for a while. I had beat him in a 2on2, and 2 1on1s, and he wanted to prove he could beat me on Sea haha. Well he comes onto Kali drunk as can be. He could barely type. It was so funny. We played 1on1 on FOC. I was Yellow, (FOCing Yellow) he was Orange. The pictures pretty much tell it all :)


I made a few Juggs and sent them to the left and above of my Shipyard, on top of Orange. I see a transport coming, my 2-3 Juggs fire and it sunk.

It seems he had attempted to rush me, but I killed his transport full of Juggs. Hahahhaha. Man that was incrible luck, bad luck on his side :) That's the only time I ever stopped an initial Grunt Invasion with Juggs :) You can see dor expressing the hoplessness of his situation now ;)



I got his poor Peons :)

Dor making empty promises :)

I decided to show him I was ahead by building my mound and first barracks on HIS island ;)

After my barracks and Mound were done, I figured I'd build a tower :)

Dor was hiding some grunts and beat down my tower. My Juggs were providing shore support :)

He then Peon Rushed me. hahaha.

Nothing like hearing the enemy admit their loss :)

A Shlonglor Original, (Well I ripped it offa G-Man) Tower in the town as they die :)



Man I think the moral is. Don't Drink and Play War 2. After I beat dor, I think everyone else in Kchat got a piece of him too hahahahha.

Don't play with Monsters^

Last Updated 3/24/96

What a LLLLLLLLLLOOOOOOOOOOOSSSSSSSSSEEEEEEEEEEERRRRRRR!

What kind of person quits a game just because their shipyard is destroyed? What a bad teammate? I say don't play with Monsters
